Greenville, Mississippi welcomes you! Greenville is the heart and soul of the Delta! This website will provide residence, visitors, and business people with a useful and comprehensive source of information.
Among the top five largest cities in the State of Mississippi, Greenville is home to many different cultural backgrounds. We have great historical sites, and proudly boast a very active arts community. Downtown Greenville is a registered Main Street community and is in the process of revitalizing its downtown area.
Friendly people, delicious food, historical sites, a thriving city government, and so much more encompass the City of Greenville.
